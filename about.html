About Me

Hello! My name is Mina Maged, and I'm an artist based in Cairo. Born on December 23, 2006, I come from a family with a rich artistic heritage. My journey in art began with the stories I heard about my grandfather, a talented artist who passed away about 20 years before I was born. Though I never had the chance to meet him, his legacy and the artwork he left behind have been a constant source of inspiration for me.

Growing up, I was fascinated by the intricacies of design and the beauty of expression. Art became more than just a hobby; it became a way to communicate and explore the depths of imagination. My primary focus has been on drawing and digital art, with a particular fondness for automotive design. There's something captivating about capturing the essence of a car, like a Porsche 911, and bringing it to life on paper.

However, the journey hasn't been without its challenges. The world of art is vast and ever-evolving, and it can sometimes feel daunting to keep up with new techniques and trends. The progress can be slow, and the path is often filled with obstacles, whether it's finding one's unique style or dealing with creative blocks. But each challenge has been a stepping stone, pushing me to refine my skills and expand my artistic horizons.

One of the hardest aspects has been balancing my studies with my passion for art. Being a high school student, I often find myself juggling academic responsibilities with the desire to create. Yet, these challenges have taught me resilience and the importance of perseverance. Every piece I create is a testament to my growth, a marker of the slow yet rewarding evolution of my craft.

As I continue my artistic journey, I aim to explore new mediums and merge my love for art with technology. The possibilities are endless, from digital illustrations to animation. My ultimate goal is to create works that resonate with people, evoke emotions, and perhaps, inspire others as my grandfather's legacy inspired me.

Thank you for visiting my website. I invite you to explore my gallery and join me on this journey of artistic exploration and growth.
