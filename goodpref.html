Guide To A Good Preference

When choosing a preference make sure that you have a high quality picture that have good distribution of shadows and highlights and it must be a sharp picture for excellent details and avoid pixilated picture to have an outrageous result.

Also make sure that color of the main component are suitable with the background if you need more contrast make sure that yo add complementary colors like blue and orange or red and green Also make sure that you've made the right texture metals will have more highlights and reflects the objects around it
